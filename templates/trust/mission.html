Our Mission

"Clean Energy in Every Home, Empowering a Sustainable Future."

At Har Ghar Solar, our mission is to accelerate the adoption of rooftop solar energy across India. We believe that every homeowner should have access to clean, affordable, and reliable electricity.

Why We Do What We Do

India is transitioning rapidly towards renewable energy. Our platform simplifies the complex process of going solar by providing transparent pricing, clear information on government subsidies, and connecting homeowners with vetted, high-quality solar installers.

Our Core Values

  • Transparency: Clear communication about costs, ROI, and installation timelines.
  • Quality: Ensuring top-tier materials and certified installers for every project.
  • Accessibility: Making solar energy understandable and affordable for all.
