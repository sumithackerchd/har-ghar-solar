How We Verify Information

Accuracy is our top priority. The solar industry and government policies change frequently, and we employ a strict verification process to ensure our content remains reliable.

1. Official Sources

All subsidy information, including PM Surya Ghar Yojana details, is sourced directly from official Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE) portals and state nodal agencies.

2. Expert Review

Our articles, calculators, and technical guides are reviewed by certified solar engineers and industry experts before publication.

3. Regular Updates

We conduct quarterly audits of all our core content to ensure that pricing, technology standards, and government schemes are up to date.
